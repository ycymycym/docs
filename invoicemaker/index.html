隐私政策 · Privacy Policy

隐私政策（中文）

最后更新：2026 年 4 月 2 日

概述

Invoice Maker（"本应用"）是一款以本地存储为核心的发票管理工具。我们不收集、不上传、不出售您的任何个人数据。您的所有业务信息均存储在您的设备本地。

我们收集什么

本应用不向任何服务器发送数据，以下信息仅存储在您的设备本地：

  • 您在商家资料中填写的名称、地址、Logo、联系方式
  • 您创建的发票、报价单、客户、费用及预约记录
  • 您配置的 SMTP 邮件账户信息（使用设备安全存储加密保存）
  • 您上传的照片（Logo、报价单附件）

相机与相册权限

本应用会在以下情况请求访问相册和相机：

  • 上传商家 Logo
  • 在报价单中附加照片

这些图片仅存储在您的设备本地，不会上传到任何服务器。

应用内购买

订阅和一次性购买通过 Apple App Store 处理。我们无法访问您的支付信息。Apple 的隐私政策适用于交易过程。

邮件发送

本应用使用您提供的 SMTP 账户直接发送邮件，不经过我们的服务器。您的邮件内容和收件人信息不会被我们收集。

数据安全

所有数据保存在设备本地数据库中。SMTP 密码等敏感信息使用 iOS Keychain（Secure Storage）加密存储。

第三方服务

本应用不集成任何广告、分析或追踪 SDK。

儿童隐私

本应用不面向 13 岁以下儿童，不会有意收集儿童个人信息。

联系我们

如有任何隐私相关问题，请通过以下邮箱联系：support@invoicemaker.app

Privacy Policy (English)

Last updated: April 2, 2026

Overview

Invoice Maker ("the App") is a local-first invoicing tool. We do not collect, transmit, or sell any of your personal data. All your business information is stored exclusively on your device.

What We Store

The App does not send any data to external servers. The following is stored locally on your device only:

  • Business profile details (name, address, logo, contact info)
  • Invoices, estimates, clients, expenses, and appointments you create
  • SMTP email credentials (encrypted via iOS Secure Storage / Keychain)
  • Photos you attach (logo, estimate attachments)

Camera & Photo Library Access

The App requests camera and photo library access solely to:

  • Upload your business logo
  • Attach photos to estimates

These images are stored on-device only and never uploaded to any server.

In-App Purchases

Subscriptions and one-time purchases are processed by Apple. We never have access to your payment information. Apple's privacy policy governs the transaction.

Email Sending

The App uses the SMTP credentials you provide to send emails directly — not through our servers. We do not collect or log the content of your emails or recipient addresses.

Data Security

All data is stored in a local encrypted database. Sensitive credentials (SMTP passwords) are stored in iOS Keychain via Secure Storage.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any advertising, analytics, or tracking SDKs.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

常见问题 / FAQ

Q: 为什么 PDF 显示中文标签？/ Why is my PDF in Chinese?
进入 More → Settings → Invoice & Payment → PDF Language，修改语言后点击 Save。新建文件将使用新语言。
Go to More → Settings → Invoice & Payment → PDF Language, change it and tap Save. Newly created documents will use the new language.
Q: 如何发送发票邮件？/ How do I send invoices by email?
进入 More → Settings → SMTP Settings，填写您的邮件服务器信息（支持 Gmail、Outlook 等）。配置完成后，打开任意发票并点击分享图标即可发送。
Go to More → Settings → SMTP Settings and enter your email server details (Gmail, Outlook, etc. supported). Then open any invoice and tap the share icon to send.
Q: 免费版有什么限制？/ What are the Free Tier limits?
免费版最多创建 5 份文件（发票或报价单合计）。升级 Pro 后无限制。
The Free Tier allows up to 5 documents (invoices + estimates combined). Upgrade to Pro for unlimited.
Q: 如何恢复购买？/ How do I restore my purchase?
进入 More → Upgrade to Pro → Restore Purchases。确保使用购买时的同一 Apple ID 登录。
Go to More → Upgrade to Pro → Restore Purchases. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.
Q: 数据会备份吗？/ Is my data backed up?
数据通过 iCloud 备份（如您已开启设备 iCloud 备份）。我们不在自己的服务器上存储任何数据。
Your data is backed up via iCloud if you have device iCloud Backup enabled. We do not store any data on our own servers.
Q: 支持哪些 PDF 模板？/ What PDF templates are available?
目前提供 5 种模板：Classic（经典紫色）、Minimal（极简灰色）、Modern（现代深色）、Warm（暖橙色）、Ocean（海洋绿色）。
5 templates available: Classic (purple), Minimal (steel), Modern (charcoal), Warm (deep orange), Ocean (teal).
Q: 如何取消订阅？/ How do I cancel my subscription?
前往 iPhone 系统设置 → Apple ID → 订阅，找到 Invoice Maker 并取消。或在 App Store 中管理订阅。
Go to iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions, find Invoice Maker and cancel. Or manage via App Store.
